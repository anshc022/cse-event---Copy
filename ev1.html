About the Event

"Are you a student with a great business idea? Join our Startup Pitch Competition! It's like being on Shark Tank – you present your idea to judges. You'll learn a lot, grow your skills, and make a big impact. Don't miss this opportunity – Register Today!"

Prizes will be awarded to the top five pitches..

Event Information

  • The event will take place on February 29th, 2024.
  • Venue: Engineering Hive (4303).
  • The contest will start at 1:30 PM.
  • Only the first 15 Teams can register.
  • Half day OD will be provided.

Rules

  • Presentation Format: Bring your PowerPoint (PPT) presentation.
  • Team Size: Keep it small – 3 members maximum per team
  • Punctuality: Be on time for your presentation slot
  • Content: Cover key points like problem-solving and market analysis
  • Time: Stick to the time limit.
  • Professionalism: Stay professional during your presentation.
  • Q&A: Be ready for questions from the judges.
  • Originality: Your idea must be original.
  • Materials: Bring any extra stuff you need.
